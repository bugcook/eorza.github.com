1.mysql启动和关闭：

    
    /usr/local/etc/rc.d/mysql-server start
    
    /usr/local/etc/rc.d/mysql-server stop
    
    /usr/local/etc/rc.d/mysql-server restart


2.apache启动和关闭：

    
    apachectl start
    
    apachectl stop
    
    apachectl restart
